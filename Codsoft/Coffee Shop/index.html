about us

what makes our coffee special?

Specially coffee is more than the work of one person. It comes from the combined efforts of everyone along the chain of production. From farmers and harvesters to roasters and brewers, specialty coffee is the result of passion, precision, and professionalism. All this work goes into producing the very finest cups of coffee, and its this loving attention to detail that makes specialty coffee so, well, special!

The unique flavours and tastes are a result of the special characteristics and composition of the soils in the soils in which they are produced
